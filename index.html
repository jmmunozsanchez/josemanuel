About me

I have been working since 2004 as Associate Professor at the Department of Psychology of the University of Cádiz (UCA). I am member of the research group "HUM554 Intelligence, Behavior & Adaptation".

I obtained my PhD Degree from the University of Seville in December 2000, supervised by Francisco Braza Lloret (Doñana Biological Station EBD-CSIC).

I received a Degree in Psychology (December 1992), from the National Distance Education University (UNED).

I have been a visiting predoctoral researcher at the Social Ethology Department of the Doñana Biological Station (November 1993 – October 1994) under the supervision of Francisco Braza Lloret.

Research interests

* Evolutionary Psychology.

* G x E interactions.

* Mothering and fathering.

* Temperamental traits.

* Childhood aggression.
